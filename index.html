How it works

Every CleanO2 soap product is made with carbon that would otherwise have been released into the atmosphere if not captured by our CarbinX™ units and turned into amazing soap products that are great for your body, the community, and the environment.

When you purchase CleanO2 soap products, you’re supporting the deployment of more CarbinX™ units around the world to capture more carbon and reduce the impact on our climate.

CarbinX Process

CarbinX

CarbinX™ attaches to natural gas heating appliances in commercial buildings. It then captures CO2 from the flue gas and turns it into a new chemical compound

Pearl Ash

The resulting chemical is called potassium carbonate (pearl ash). It helps our soap provide a silky-smooth lather.

Primium Soap Products

We combine the captured carbon with our high-quality soap formulations to create a handmade soap unlike any other.

Did you know that 1 CarbinX™ unit will sequester 6 to 8 tonnes of CO2 per year, which is roughly the equivalent of 300 trees over that same time period?
